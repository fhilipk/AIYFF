# About

The Adelaide International youth Film Festival is held annually and exists to encourage and showcase the creativity of young filmmakers.

The event is open to young people ages 5 to 25 years and film makers who target this age range. Along with film screenings it offers mentorship and guidance. The Festival will be a feast of screenings, workshops and Q&Aâ€™s with special invited guests

With a focus on international connections the festival fosters connection and sharing through film.
